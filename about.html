Who We Are

ALPHA Mechanics is a modern automotive service platform designed to connect vehicle owners with trusted mechanics quickly and efficiently. We specialize in mobile repair services, emergency assistance, and connecting you to the nearest service centers.

Founded with the goal of making car repair simple and stress-free, we focus on speed, reliability, and professionalism.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission:

To provide fast, reliable, and accessible mechanic services to drivers anytime, anywhere.

Vision:

To become the leading mobile mechanic service platform in Africa, known for trust, efficiency, and innovation.

What We Do

  • We connect users to nearby mechanics in minutes

  • We provide emergency roadside assistance 24/7

  • We offer mobile mechanic services at your location

  • We support diagnostics, repairs, and towing solutions

Why Choose Us

  1. ⚡ Fast response time

  2. 🛠️ Skilled and verified mechanics

  3. 📍 Wide coverage across multiple regions

  4. 🕒 24/7 availability

  5. 🤝 Reliable and transparent service

Our Coverage Area

We currently serve major regions across Kenya including Nairobi, Coastal Region, North Eastern, Western, and Nyanza.

We are continuously expanding to reach more drivers nationwide.
